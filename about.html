About Me

My name is Brandon Seymour. I currently work in healthcare IT as a Senior Desktop Support Analyst and recently completed my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Southern New Hampshire University.

Prior to entering the IT field, I served in the United States Marine Corps as a helicopter mechanic. My professional experience has primarily focused on systems administration, infrastructure support, networking, endpoint management, and technical troubleshooting.

Through the Computer Science program, I developed skills in software engineering, algorithms and data structures, databases, graphics programming, secure coding, and software testing. My long-term goal is to transition into software engineering leadership and continue growing my development and cybersecurity expertise.
