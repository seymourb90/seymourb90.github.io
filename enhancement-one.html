Enhancement One: Software Design and Engineering

This enhancement focuses on improving the software design, organization, and maintainability of my original CS330 Computational Graphics project. The primary enhancement involved refactoring a large RenderScene() method into smaller reusable rendering functions and moving lighting configuration into its own dedicated method.

These changes improved readability, modularity, and future maintainability while preserving the original functionality of the project.
